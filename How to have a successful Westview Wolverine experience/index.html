Important things to Remember

Always remember to do things on time, procrastinating is not worth the stress of fixing your grades later on. Figure out early on what your teachers expectations are, what they like, do they prefer a more creative approach to things, or maybe they prefer you to just be straight forward. While grades are important, make sure you balance school and your personal life, your body and mind will thank you.
